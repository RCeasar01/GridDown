Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

GridDown is built for the worst moments. Every feature works without internet, without cloud, without your data.

🩹
TCCC Medical Guides
Trauma care, wound treatment, field medicine, and preventive care written by Eagle First Responders. Real procedures for when no doctor is coming.
🗺️
Offline GPS Maps
Display your exact GPS coordinates without any network connection. Works in dead zones, wilderness, and grid-down scenarios.
🤖
AI Field Advisor
A large language model that runs entirely on your device. No internet, no servers, no data leaving your phone. Available on Extreme plan.
🌐
Offline Translator
Translate to/from 10 languages entirely on-device. Spanish, French, Portuguese, German, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Hindi. Emergency phrases pre-loaded for instant large-font display.
📡
HAM Radio Repeater DB
200+ US repeater entries covering all 50 states. Filter by OPEN/ARES/RACES/SKYWARN. Search by state, city, or frequency. Fully offline.
•−
Morse Code Tool
Encode and decode Morse code in real time. Visual flash playback. SOS shortcut. Full international Morse table. Works without any radio or signal.
🪖
US Army Field Manuals
Condensed from public-domain FM 21-76 and allied manuals: urban survival, map & compass navigation, field sanitation, immediate action drills, Combat Lifesaver extended care.
🚗
Vehicle & Mechanical
Tire change without a jack, jump-starting, engine overheating, fuel siphoning, and generator maintenance. Real technical content for field emergencies.
🌾
Homesteading & Food
Raising chickens, emergency gardening, food preservation without electricity, rainwater collection, and livestock butchering basics.
☑️
Smart Checklists
72-hour kits, bug-out bags, shelter-in-place protocols, and more. Check off items and track your preparedness — all stored locally.
🌍
10-Language Support
Fully localized UI in English, Spanish, French, Portuguese, German, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, and Hindi. On-device translation for guide content.
🔒
Zero Data Collection
We collect nothing. No account. No analytics. No location tracking. Your data stays on your device, protected by your OS security.
New in v2.0

The biggest update ever.

15 major UX and content improvements — from a one-tap emergency crisis UI to personalized onboarding and real-world scenario flows.

🆘
Emergency Mode
One tap from HomeScreen. Strips the app to crisis-only UI: medical micro cards, survival priorities, SOS signaling, disaster checklists, and live GPS coordinates. Minimum 20px fonts. Minimum 64px tap targets.
🎒
My Kit
Build custom gear kits — Bug-Out Bag, Vehicle Kit, Home Kit, Medic Bag. Pin any guide to any kit. Instant access from Emergency Mode and the Tools tab.
🌊
Real-World Flows
6 guided scenario sequences: Bug-Out in 15 Minutes, Shelter-in-Place 72 Hours, Vehicle Breakdown Remote, Post-Storm Assessment, Medical Emergency No EMS, Nuclear/EMP First Hour. Step-by-step with per-step checklists.
🌙
Night Ops Mode
Ultra-dark #050505 background, dimmed orange accent, all animations disabled. Preserves night vision. Toggle from HomeScreen header or Settings.
⚡
Quick Glance Summaries
Every guide now has a Quick Glance: situation context, 3 immediate priorities in 18px bold orange, and follow-up steps. See the critical actions before reading the full guide.
🎯
Personalization Presets
4-screen onboarding selects your profile (Urban, Rural, Medic, Vehicle, Comms, Disaster) and region. Category order, pinned checklists, and daily drills all personalized to your scenario.
