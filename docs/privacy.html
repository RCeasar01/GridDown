Privacy Policy

Effective: June 19, 2026 — BannedProduct Media Inc.

1. Overview

GridDown is built on a single privacy principle: we do not collect your data. The app functions entirely offline. No account is required. No data leaves your device for GridDown’s servers — because GridDown has no servers that receive personal data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

GridDown does not collect, transmit, sell, or share: your name, email, location, browsing history, device identifiers, advertising IDs, biometric data, or crash reports.

4. Location Data

GridDown requests location permission to display GPS coordinates on the offline map. Location data is displayed on screen only — never stored, logged, or transmitted. It is accessed only on the Map screen and only while you’re on it.

5. Payments

All purchases are handled by RevenueCat and the platform payment processor (Apple or Google). GridDown never sees your payment information. RevenueCat may process an anonymous app user ID for purchase verification. See RevenueCat’s privacy policy.

6. On-Device AI

The Field Intelligence Advisor (Extreme plan) runs entirely on your device. Your conversations are never transmitted anywhere. They exist in device memory during the session and are cleared when you close the chat.

7. Translation

Language model files (∼30 MB each) are downloaded from Google’s MLKit servers. Once downloaded, all translation occurs on-device with no network calls. Translated text is cached in your local SQLite database.

8. Analytics

GridDown integrates no analytics SDK whatsoever. No Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no similar service. Apple and Google may collect standard platform diagnostics per their own policies.

10. Children’s Privacy

GridDown does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. No account creation is required at any age.

11. Your Rights

Because we do not collect your data on our servers, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or export. For any questions, email privacy@bannedproduct.com.

12. Changes

Material changes will be noted in the app’s update release notes with an updated effective date above.
