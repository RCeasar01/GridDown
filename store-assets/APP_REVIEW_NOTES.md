## 1. Medical Content — App Store Guideline 5.1.1

GridDown includes emergency preparedness guides covering field medicine (wound care, hypothermia management, improvised splinting, burn treatment, shock protocol, etc.). This content is intended for emergency situations where **professional care is unavailable** — grid-down scenarios, natural disasters, wilderness emergencies, and austere environments.

### Founder Credentials

Content is authored by a founder with the following verified background:
- **Combat Infantryman** — U.S. Army, 101st Airborne Division (Air Assault)
- **Eagle First Responder** — combat lifesaver certification, 101st Airborne
- **Preventive Medicine Specialist** (MOS 68S) — trained in field sanitation, disease prevention, and public health in austere environments
- **Veteran** with direct operational experience using this knowledge in the field

### How the Medical Disclaimer Is Implemented

Every guide with `category: 'medical'` or `requiresMedicalDisclaimer: true` renders a **non-dismissable inline disclaimer banner** as the **first visible content element**, before any procedural steps. The disclaimer reads:

> ⚕ MEDICAL INFORMATION — EMERGENCY REFERENCE ONLY  
> This guide is intended for emergency use when professional medical care is unavailable. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek qualified medical care whenever possible.

The banner is:
- **Inline** (not a modal, not a bottom sheet — cannot be skipped)
- **Non-dismissable** (no close button, no dismiss gesture)
- **Rendered before Step 1** in the guide content flow

## 2. Field Intelligence AI Feature

### What It Is

"Field Intelligence" is an on-device AI advisor powered by **Phi-3.5 Mini Instruct (Q4_K_M quantization, ~2.2 GB)** via the `llama.rn` library.

### Privacy & Data

- **No data is transmitted to external servers.** All inference runs locally on the device.
- The model file is downloaded once from Hugging Face (user-initiated download, user's choice of timing).
- After download, the feature operates with **zero internet connection required**.
- No conversation history is stored in the cloud, logged, or transmitted.
- No user identification is associated with queries.

### Disclosure in the App

Per Apple's 2024 AI disclosure requirement:
- Every AI-generated response is labeled **"◈ FIELD INTELLIGENCE · AI-ASSISTED"** in the message bubble.
- A persistent disclosure banner appears at the top of the chat interface: *"Field Intelligence responses are generated by an AI model running on your device. Always verify critical survival information with qualified professionals when possible."*
- The same disclosure is shown on the model download screen before the user downloads anything.

### Feature Gate

Field Intelligence is available exclusively to **Extreme tier** subscribers (Extreme Monthly, Extreme Yearly, Extreme Lifetime).

## 3. Location Permission

GridDown requests `NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription` only. Location usage:
- Reads the device GPS to **display current coordinates** on the offline map screen.
- Location data is **never stored, never transmitted, never shared** with third parties.
- No background location access.
- No geofencing.

## 5. Offline Architecture

GridDown is designed to work **100% offline** after initial content sync:
- 60+ guides bundled as static JSON assets in the app bundle.
- Offline maps via local tile caching.
- AI model stored locally after user-initiated download.
- RevenueCat entitlement state cached locally (works offline).
- No account system. No login required. App works on first launch without any network access.
