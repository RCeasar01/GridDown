GRIDDOWN IS THE SURVIVAL GUIDE BUILT FOR WHEN INFRASTRUCTURE FAILS.

No internet required. No cloud dependency. Every piece of content — water procurement, shelter construction, medical triage, security protocols, navigation, communications — is stored on-device and accessible the moment you need it.

WHAT'S INSIDE

► 60+ Field Guides across 10 categories
Written to military and wilderness medicine standards. Not listicles. Not watered-down blog content. Actionable, step-by-step protocols with warnings, pro tips, and field-verified techniques.

► 10 Survival Categories
Water · Fire · Shelter · Food · Medical · Navigation · Communications · Security · Tools · Disaster Response

► Offline-First Architecture
Zero network calls for content. Open the app anywhere — no bars, no WiFi, no service — and every guide is instantly available.

► Smart Search
Full-text search across all guides, with results ranked by criticality. Find what you need in seconds, not minutes.

► Tactical Checklists
Pre-built and customizable survival checklists for bug-out, shelter-in-place, vehicle kit, and 72-hour scenarios. Check off items as you execute.

► Bookmark System
Save guides to instant-access bookmarks. Your most-used protocols are always one tap away.

► Field Intelligence AI (Extreme Tier)
On-device AI advisor powered by a local language model. Ask tactical questions and get context-aware survival guidance — completely offline, no data leaves your device.

DESIGNED FOR

• Preppers and survivalists who want depth, not fluff
• Outdoorsmen, hunters, and backcountry travelers
• First responders, military, and veterans who want a portable field reference
• Anyone who believes infrastructure can fail and wants to be ready

SUBSCRIPTION TIERS

Free — Access to critical-priority guides across all categories. No account required.
Discord — Community access + all free content.
Monthly / Yearly — Full guide library including advanced and specialized content.
Extreme — Everything above plus on-device AI Field Intelligence advisor.

All content is accessible without an account. Premium tiers unlock the full library and AI advisor. Restore purchases available at any time.

PRIVACY

GridDown does not collect personal data, does not require account creation for free content, and does not transmit guide content to any server. The AI advisor runs entirely on your device. See our privacy policy for details.

MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

Medical guides in GridDown are intended for use in austere environments where trained medical care is unavailable. They do not replace professional medical training. Seek qualified instruction in first aid and tactical medicine.
